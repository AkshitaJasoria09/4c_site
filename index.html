ABOUT

Marketing’s job is never done. It’s about perpetual motion. We must continue to innovate every day,” is a quote that strongly resonates with us.

4C - The Marketing Cell of MPSTME, NMIMS University was formed in 2008 with the aim of imparting marketing knowledge to students through myriads of lively events and by finding an ideal balance between informative and fun events throughout the year, this marketing cell has created a very distinct reputation among its peers.

Coming together is a beginning. Keeping together is progress. Working together is success. Here at 4C, we emphasize a great deal on team bonding and making connections that will last for a lifetime. We have been successful in creating an environment which follows the pomodoro technique efficiently, where we not only focus on work but also ensure to have fun while working. We encourage our team members to not stick within the borders of the “approved” output and be open towards new, fun ideas.

This marketing cell provides its members with ample creative freedom which encourages them to always try and learn something new. Be it an offline or an online platform, 4C has always been successful at organizing well known events. With events like D-Talks, Wings and Roots and Debattle, it is evident that we, here at 4C have been firm believers of increasing knowledge by sharing it. Whereas events like Trapped, Roulette, Bombae Square and Emporia is proof that we do not lack on the fun side of the spectrum either.
