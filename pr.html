EDITORIAL

Public relations is the committee that the entirety of 4C relies on. All of our events would be incomplete without PR. They bring in the people in, which is the whole and soul purpose of marketing and collar the attention that our events require and deserve. From sending messages to making sure that the registrations are lined up perfectly for our events, PR does it all. It is heart-warming to see how welcoming they are, during times of intense marketing and publicity almost everybody wants to be a part of their process. Their friendship is worth envying and their work ethics are inspiring. With the right spirit and a close knit bond PR is all set and ready to bring all events to life.
